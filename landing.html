The Hot Tamale

Discover a world of fiery flavors and reward your adventurous taste buds. Find sizzling hotspots serving the spiciest culinary delights near you, conveniently displayed on an interactive map

  • View Website

Description

The Hot Tamale was born out of a desire to connect like-minded spice enthusiasts with their favorite flavors while also demonstrating my skills in front-end development, API integration, and creating user-friendly interfaces. I use this app often in my hometown of Loveland, CO and have discovered my favorite Mexican (El Cielo), Indian (Durbar Bistro) and Thai (Bai-Tong) restaurants. Try it yourself!
