About Me

I'm a full-stack developer from Loveland, Colorado, educated at the University of Colorado and LeWagon Coding Bootcamp. I specialize in crafting visually captivating websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, while also integrating back-end functionalities through RESTful APIs and working with SQL/NoSQL databases like PostgreSQL and MongoDB. My version control experience on GitHub allows me to seamlessly work alongside fellow developers, contributing to projects, tracking changes, and ensuring a smooth process of collaboration. Beyond coding, I'm an avid guitar player, Spanish language learner, scuba diver, and spicy food enthusiast, blending my diverse interests into creating immersive web experiences.
